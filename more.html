More about me.

My main research areas gather Pervasive Computing, Internet of Things, Semantic Web, Adaptive Semantic Middleware, Linked Open Data, Social Data Mining, and specially Adaptive User Interfaces. In 2015 I have been honored with the PhD recognition thanks to my dissertation entitled "Dynamic User Interface Adaptation Engine Through Semantic Modelling and Reasoning for Mobile Devices", advisored by Dr. Diego López de Ipiña and Dr. Aitor Almeida.

I finished my degree on Computer Engineering in 2008 at the University of Deusto. Since then, I've kept "training" myself through the last years, finishing a MSc in Software Development in 2010 and my PhD in 2015 at the same university.

Awards

  • In 2008 I was honored with the Best Student Final Degree Project in Computer Engineering by the University of Deusto for "SMIT - Desing and implementation of traffic violations monitoring system".
  • In March 7, 2012, I was honored again with the Via Inteligente Awards with several colleges thanks to the AssistedCity project (as a demonstrator of the Imhotep user interface adaptation framework).
